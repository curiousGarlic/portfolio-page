About me

Jon Aziz

Github Linkedin FreeCodeCamp Codepen

Creating striking interactive visual experiences gives me a huge dopamine kick.
I can work non-stop until my creation comes to life. If it does not make me smile and say “Looks and works awesome!”, I continue refining it.
Software engineering is a perfect blend of science and art. To be great, every web project demands scientific precision and creative, artistic vision. This demand inspires me and drives me ever forward.
In web development, my best allies are HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I try to get “under the hood” of browsers and devices to see their potential and limitations. That helps me optimize the visuals and performance.
Apart from web development, I love independent learning and adaptive research. Learning new tools quickly and deeply and building on best practices help push my limits.
I put quality before quantity and always try to give more than I take.

Strong trees grow out of strong seeds. When they grow together, they create jungles that give life to the Earth. I would love to become a part of a cool jungle. To create a ton of awesome.
Let’s create that awesome together!
